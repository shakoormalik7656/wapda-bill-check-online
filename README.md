# WAPDA Online Bill Check

A streamlined, modern, and highly secure web utility for checking electricity bills across all major distribution companies (DISCOs) in Pakistan.

**Live Project & Official Website:** [WAPDA Online Bill Check](https://www.wapdaonlinebillcheck.com/)

## Supported Electrical Providers
- **LESCO**: Lahore Electric Supply Company
- **GEPCO**: Gujranwala Electric Power Company
- **FESCO**: Faisalabad Electric Supply Company
- **IESCO**: Islamabad Electric Supply Company
- **MEPCO**: Multan Electric Power Company
- **PESCO**: Peshawar Electric Supply Company
- **HAZECO**: Hazara Electric Supply Company
- **HESCO**: Hyderabad Electric Supply Company

## Key Features
- **Instant Retrieval**: Fetch duplicate bills securely by providing your 14-digit reference number.
- **Official Database Integration**: Accurate URL redirection to authoritative PITC servers.
- **Privacy First**: Fully stateless architecture—no personal logging or PII storage.
- **Mobile First**: Tailored responsive design for seamless viewing and PDF downloading.
